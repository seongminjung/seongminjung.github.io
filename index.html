Bio

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Electrical & Electronic Engineering at Yonsei University, South Korea. I am expecting to obtain my bachelor's degree in February 2024. I spent a year as an exchange student at Case Western Reserve University, Ohio, from August 2022 to May 2023. Currently, I am interning as a full-time research assistant at Mobiltech, mainly focusing on multi-robot Visual SLAM. I have a strong interest in autonomous mobile robotics, and my research motivation is to create robotic solutions for real-world challenges.
