Bio

I am a master's student at Robot Vision Lab at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. Jongwoo Lim. My research interests are broadly in 3D Vision, Mobile Robotics, and Generative AI. I obtained my bachelor's degree in Electrical & Electronic Engineering at Yonsei University, where I graduated with High Honors.

I was an exchange student at Case Western Reserve University, where I developed a strong interest in AI-powered robotic softwares. After graduating Yonsei, I interned as a full-time research assistant at Mobiltech, mainly focusing on multi-robot SLAM.

My vision is to empower robots to navigate and interact with the world even better than we humans do.
