Bio

I am a second-year master's student at Robot Vision Lab at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. Jongwoo Lim. My research interests are broadly in 3D Vision and Robotics. I obtained my bachelor's degree in Electrical & Electronic Engineering at Yonsei University, where I graduated with High Honors.

My vision is to empower robots to navigate and interact with the world even better than we humans do.
