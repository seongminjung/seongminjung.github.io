Bio

I am a second-year master's student at Robot Vision Lab at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. Jongwoo Lim. My research interests are broadly in 3D Vision, Scene Understanding, Robotics, and Generative Models. I obtained my bachelor's degree in Electrical & Electronic Engineering at Yonsei University, where I graduated with High Honors.

I was an exchange student at Case Western Reserve University, where I participated in the robotics club, CWRUBotix, and developed a strong interest in AI-powered robotic softwares. After graduating Yonsei, I interned as a full-time research assistant at Mobiltech, mainly focusing on multi-robot SLAM using Isaac Sim.

My vision is to empower robots to navigate and interact with the world even better than we humans do.
